About AutoPDF Cloud v6.3

AutoPDF Cloud is a completely free web application that helps people around the world create professional PDF documents and automate repetitive tasks — with no cost, no registration, and no limitations.

No sign-up. No watermarks. No hidden fees. Just simple, powerful tools for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality document tools, no matter where they live or how much money they have. A student in a small town, a teacher in a big city, a small business owner, or a volunteer — all deserve the same professional results.

That’s why AutoPDF Cloud is built to be easy to use, fast, and available in the world’s most spoken languages.

What We Offer

Our platform includes two powerful tools:

  • PDF Generator: Turn any text into a clean, professional PDF with perfect formatting
  • Automation Bot: Create hundreds of personalized documents using a simple template and data list
  • 10 Languages: Use the tool in English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, French, Arabic, Bengali, Russian, Japanese, or German
  • No File Storage: Your documents are created instantly and never saved on our servers
  • Works on Any Device: Use it on your phone, tablet, or computer — no app needed

How It Works

Using AutoPDF Cloud is simple:

  1. Choose your language
  2. Type your text or upload a file
  3. Click "Generate" to download your PDF
  4. Or use the bot to create many documents at once

No software to install. No account to create. Just open your browser and start.

Privacy and Trust

We respect your privacy completely. When you use AutoPDF Cloud:

  • Your files are processed in a secure, temporary space
  • Nothing is saved after you download
  • We don’t ask for your name, email, or any personal information
  • No tracking or ads in your documents

Why We Stay Free

We keep AutoPDF Cloud free by showing a small, respectful ad banner at the bottom of the page. This ad never appears in your PDFs and helps us keep the service running for everyone.
