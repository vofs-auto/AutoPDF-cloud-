Welcome to AutoPDF Cloud. This Cookies Policy explains how and why cookies are used on our website. By continuing to browse or use AutoPDF Cloud, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. This document provides detailed information about what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage or disable them if you choose to do so.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track how you interact with them, and improve your overall browsing experience. Some cookies are necessary for the site to function, while others are used for analytics or advertising.

2. How AutoPDF Cloud Uses Cookies

AutoPDF Cloud uses cookies primarily to improve your user experience and maintain website functionality. Since our service is free and supported by advertising through Google AdSense, certain cookies may also be used to deliver relevant ads. These cookies do not personally identify you but help display content that is more suitable to your interests.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the proper operation of the website, allowing you to generate PDFs, count active users, and maintain session stability.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site and which features are most popular. This allows us to improve the service and user interface.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display either personalized or non-personalized ads. These cookies may collect anonymous information such as your device type, general location, or browsing patterns.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your chosen theme (dark or light mode) or language preferences so that AutoPDF Cloud remembers your settings during your next visit.

3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Cookies

AutoPDF Cloud uses Google AdSense to display ads and maintain the service free of charge. Google may use cookies or web beacons to serve relevant ads based on your previous interactions with websites. These cookies are controlled by Google, and AutoPDF Cloud does not have direct access to or control over them. You can review Google’s own policies here: Google Advertising Policies.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can accept or decline cookies through the banner shown when you first visit AutoPDF Cloud. You may also change your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may affect how certain parts of the site work, including PDF generation and file uploads.

5. Do We Store Personal Data?

No, AutoPDF Cloud does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. Uploaded PDFs and text content are processed temporarily and deleted automatically after use. We value user privacy and transparency above all.

When you first visit AutoPDF Cloud, a banner appears at the bottom of the page asking for your consent to use cookies. By clicking “Accept,” you agree to the use of cookies described in this policy. If you do not agree, you may decline or disable cookies in your browser settings.
