Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 29, 2025. AutoPDF Cloud (the "Service") respects the privacy of visitors and users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we process it, and the rights you have regarding your data. This policy applies to the Service at https://autopdf-cloud-awgq.onrender.com.

Summary: AutoPDF Cloud is designed to operate with minimal data collection. We do not require user accounts to use the core features, and uploaded documents and generated PDFs are processed temporarily for the purpose of generating output and are not kept permanently on our servers.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to provide the service:

  • Uploaded files: When you upload a PDF for text extraction, we process the file server-side to extract text and deliver results. Files are deleted automatically shortly after processing.
  • Generated files: PDFs generated by the service are offered as direct downloads and are not retained permanently by default.
  • Technical logs: Standard server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, user agent) may be stored briefly for debugging and abuse prevention. These logs are purged regularly.
  • Analytics and ads: If third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdSense) are used, they may collect non-identifying browsing information as described by their own policies. We do not control the data practices of third parties; consult their policies for details.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Processing uploaded files and returning extracted text to the user.
  • Generating PDFs requested by users and delivering them as downloads.
  • Monitoring service health, diagnosing issues, and preventing abuse or misuse.
  • Displaying relevant advertising to help maintain the service (advertising networks may set cookies).

Data Retention and Deletion

Files uploaded to AutoPDF Cloud are processed in a temporary workspace and are removed automatically after processing. Generated PDFs are provided as direct downloads; they are not stored permanently on the server unless explicitly offered as a feature. Server logs used for debugging or security are retained for a limited time and purged regularly.

Cookies and Third-Party Services

AutoPDF Cloud itself uses minimal client-side storage. If you or the site owner integrates third-party widgets, analytics, or ads (such as Google AdSense), those providers may set cookies or use other tracking mechanisms. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. The site should be deployed over HTTPS in production. While no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, we strive to use reasonable safeguards consistent with common hosting providers and modern security practices.

Children

AutoPDF Cloud is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.
