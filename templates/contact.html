Contact Us

We want to hear from you. AutoPDF Cloud is a small, focused service, and direct feedback is the most effective way to improve. Whether you have a feature request, a bug report, a partnership idea, or a question about privacy or ads, the best way to reach us is by email at vofs.auto@gmail.com.

Support & Feedback

Please include as much detail as possible in your message: a short description of the issue, the browser and device you are using, the steps to reproduce a bug (if any), and screenshots if relevant. For feature requests, an explanation of your use case helps prioritize development.

Business and Partnerships

If you represent a business, educational institution, or developer team and are interested in licensing, white-labeling, or integrating AutoPDF Cloud into a workflow, please email a short proposal. Include your company name, a brief description of the project, and the expected usage volume.

Press and Media

Media inquiries and interview requests are welcome. We can provide a short background on the project, development timeline, and information about future roadmaps. Contact details: vofs.auto@gmail.com.

Response Time

As a single-developer project, response times vary. Typical replies to support and feedback arrive within a few business days. For urgent or time-sensitive issues, include the word "URGENT" in the subject line.

Privacy & Safety Notes

When contacting us, do not send sensitive personal information such as passwords, government ID numbers, or full financial details. If you need help with document content that includes sensitive data, redact that information before sending a sample.

Additional Resources

For quick help, check the About and Privacy pages. Many common questions, including how batch generation works and how the ad model functions, are answered in those pages. If you need a screenshot or recorded clip to show the issue, small videos (under 10MB) attached to your email are helpful.

Thank you for using AutoPDF Cloud. Your feedback keeps the project alive and helps shape future improvements.
